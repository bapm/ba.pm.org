Tech-Social meeting

26 Mar 2009 18:00

(SK) Program stretnutia bol nasledový:

  • objednávka pizze
  • ukážka libchamplain (Emmanuel)
  • použitie XML::LibXML::Reader+XML::Compile::Schema (Jozef)

Pizza tentoraz prišla na čas a nezostali sme hladný! Emmanuel predviedol projekt libchamplain a jeho Perl C-bindings pomocou ktorých sa dá do GTK+ aplikácie zakomponovať mapa. Jozef ukázal ako pomocou XML::LibXML::Reader +XML::Compile::Schema spracoval 11MB xml súbor.

(EN) This was a program of the meeting:

  • pizza ordering
  • demonstration of libchamplain (Emmanuel)
  • XML::LibXML::Reader+XML::Compile::Schema example usage (Jozef)

This time pizza arrived on time and we were not hungry! Emmanuel showed us project libchamplain and his Perl C-bindings that could be used to have maps in the GTK+ application. Jozef showed how he using XML::LibXML::Reader +XML::Compile::Schema parsed a 11MB xml file.

Tech-Social meeting

19 Feb 2009 18:00

(SK) V skratke - http://search.cpan.org/perldoc?SVGraph. Na http://svn.comsultia.com/svgraph/ nájdete pôvodnú verziu a na CPAN-e si môžete stiahnuť .tar.gz. .pm súbory (okrem komentárov) neboli zmenené. Pribudli Build.PL, Changes, MANIFEST, MANIFEST.SKIP, t/ a examples/ na vytvorenie CPAN balíčka. Výsledné grafy z príkladov sa dajú pozrieť tu: http://cpansearch.perl.org/src/COMSULTIA/SVGraph-0.02/doc/src/images/graph.svg, http://cpansearch.perl.org/src/COMSULTIA/SVGraph-0.02/doc/src/images/graph2.svg.

(EN) Shortly - http://search.cpan.org/perldoc?SVGraph. In http://svn.comsultia.com/svgraph/ you can find the original version and on CPAN you can download .tar.gz. .pm files (besides comments) were not changed. Build.PL, Changes, MANIFEST, MANIFEST.SKIP, t/ and examples/ were added to create a package. Here you can see the two example graphs: http://cpansearch.perl.org/src/COMSULTIA/SVGraph-0.02/doc/src/images/graph.svg, http://cpansearch.perl.org/src/COMSULTIA/SVGraph-0.02/doc/src/images/graph2.svg.
